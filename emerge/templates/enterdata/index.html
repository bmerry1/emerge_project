About EMERGE

EMERGE Connecticut, Inc. began in 2010 as Empower Enterprises, LLC - a pilot initiative launched under Empower New Haven, Inc. The program was created to provide male and female offenders entering or re-entering the workforce with applied skills training and paid work opportunities along with the educational and life skill supports that better position them on their journey to attain stable employment and economic independence.
